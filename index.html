Visualizing Climate Change

How Climate Change Affects the World Around us

By Brennan Kauffman and Arden Bastia

The impacts of a changing climate really hit home when they, well, hit home. Here on the East Coast, we're susceptible to extreme precipitation, sea level rise, coastal flooding, and extreme heat waves (according to the U.S. Climate Resilience Toolkit). But how has climate change impacted the world beyond our backyard, neighborhood, or community? Using data from the (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration and NASA, etc.), here's a visual look at how a changing climate has impacted parts of the world from sea life in Maine to icebergs in the Arctic, providing different ways to understand our changing world.
